## Crude Oil Variants - The Game Changer ðŸŽ¯

**Total Impact:** 1,870 records, **79.1M tons** (74.5% of Phase 10 total!)

### Grades Captured:
- **BASRAH Heavy/Light** (Iraq)
- **KIRKUK** (Iraq)
- **HIBERNIA** (Canada)
- **QUA IBOE** (Nigeria)
- **PAYARA GOLD** (Guyana)
- **UNITY GOLD** (South Sudan)
- **LIZA** (Guyana)
- **TUPI** (Brazil)
- **FRADE** (Brazil)
- **ESCALANTE** (Mexico)
- **SARIR/MESLA** (Libya)
- **MAYA, MEREY, BOSCAN** (Latin America)
- **ORIENTE** (Ecuador)

**Why This Matters:**
- Crude oil is misclassified in generic petroleum HS codes
- Specific grade names are unique identifiers (100% accurate)
- Average shipment size: ~42,000 tons (very large bulk tankers)

**Lesson:** Commodity-specific grade names are more reliable than HS codes for bulk commodities.

## LPG (Propane/Butane) - ROB Cargo

**Total Impact:** 539 records, **4.0M tons**

### Pattern:
- Keywords: "PROPANE REMAINING ON BOARD", "BUTANE REMAINING ON BOARD", "ROB"
- Typical descriptions: "(274.446 MT / 3,431 BBLS) REMAINING ON BOARD FOR FOREIGN EXPORT"
- Package type: BBL (barrels) or BLK (bulk)

**Why Important:**
- ROB (Remaining On Board) cargo is transshipment/FROB cargo
- Still counted in import statistics but not offloaded at US port
- Large tonnages (avg 7,379 tons/record)

**Lesson:** "Remaining on board" and "for foreign export" are key patterns for transshipment cargo.
