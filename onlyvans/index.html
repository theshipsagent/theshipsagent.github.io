The idea

A camper, a map, and no fixed plan

OnlyVans Panama started with one converted van and a simple belief: the best of Panama is off the main road. We run relaxed, small-group trips where the route bends to the weather, the surf and the people you meet along the way.

From the Caribbean cayes of Bocas del Toro to the cloud forests of Boquete, every trip is built around going slow and seeing more.
