Investment Thesis

Strategic deep-water industrial site at epicenter of $60+ billion regional development, offering superior Gulf access and multimodal connectivity. The Port Sulphur facility combines remediated brownfield status, existing dock infrastructure (52' draft capability), and 106.99 acres of Industrial District 3-zoned property at Mississippi River Mile Marker 39—the closest deep-water terminals to open water on the entire Mississippi River system.

✓ Deepest draft capability in Plaquemines Parish
