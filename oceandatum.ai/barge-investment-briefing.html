Executive Summary

Key Findings

Comprehensive forecasting system achieving 22.4% MAPE across seven Mississippi River segments, with demonstrated ROI exceeding 350% for major shippers handling 1M+ tons annually.

Industry Scale

500+ million tons transported annually on Mississippi River System. 60% of U.S. grain exports transit this critical infrastructure, generating $7 billion in annual freight revenue and supporting 500,000+ jobs across 38 states.

Model Performance

VAR(3) and SpVAR models achieve 8.9% MAPE for 1-week forecasts, with 67.3% variance explained (R² = 0.673). Spatial correlation of 92% enables cross-segment predictions with proven statistical significance.

Business Value

Major traders (1M tons/year) realize $880K annual savings with enhanced models. 5-year NPV of $3.09M at 10% discount rate. Break-even volume: 160,000 tons annually.

Critical Market Insight

Barge rates exhibit extreme volatility, ranging from $5-135/ton with 600% spikes during drought conditions. The 2012 and 2022-2023 droughts resulted in rates exceeding $90-135/ton vs. normal $15-18/ton, creating urgent demand for predictive intelligence. Lock delays cost the industry an estimated $1.3 billion annually, with single-day delays reaching $3,000-5,000 per tow.

Forecasting Methodology

Vector Autoregressive (VAR) and Spatial VAR (SpVAR) models with 23 years of historical data.

Data Foundation

  • 1,205 weekly observations spanning 2003-2026
  • 7 river segments tracked simultaneously
  • 8,435 segment-week rate observations
  • 940 weeks training (2003-2020)
  • 265 weeks testing (2021-2026)
